Ding Chuan

Sports Journalist

Ding Chuan is a sports journalist with over ten years of experience covering major events, with a focus on tennis. He has reported on the 2017 and 2021 National Games, the 2023 Asian Games in Hangzhou, and the 2024 Paris Olympics, alongside tennis events including the 2017 Australian Open, 2018 Wimbledon, and the 2018 and 2019 WTA Finals. He is currently pursuing a Master's degree in AIDM at Hong Kong Baptist University, looking to combine his reporting background with new directions in digital media.
